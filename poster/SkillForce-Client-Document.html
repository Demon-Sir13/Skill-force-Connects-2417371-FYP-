Section 01

Project Overview

SkillForce Connects is a modern, web-based SaaS platform purpose-built for Nepal's workforce market. It creates a centralized digital marketplace where organizations — hospitals, hotels, corporate offices, event companies, and security agencies — can discover, evaluate, and hire verified skilled service providers for roles in cleaning, security, catering, maintenance, IT, healthcare, and more.

Nepal's manpower hiring process has historically been fragmented, informal, and opaque. Organizations rely on word-of-mouth referrals, phone calls, and unverified intermediaries — leading to poor matches, wasted time, and lack of accountability. SkillForce solves this by digitizing the entire hiring lifecycle: from job posting and intelligent provider matching, through application management and digital contracts, to real-time communication and NPR-based payments via Khalti and eSewa.

The Core Problem We Solve

Organizations in Nepal spend an average of 2–4 weeks finding reliable service providers through informal channels, with no verification, no contracts, and no accountability. SkillForce reduces this to hours — with verified profiles, AI-powered matching, and a structured digital workflow from hire to payment.

Platform Users & Roles

SkillForce operates on a three-role architecture, each with dedicated dashboards and permissions:

🏢

Organization (Client)

  • Post job listings with requirements
  • Browse and filter service providers
  • Review applications with AI scores
  • Generate and sign digital contracts
  • Manage payments via Khalti / eSewa
  • Access analytics dashboard
👷

Service Provider (Worker)

  • Create detailed professional profile
  • Upload CV, portfolio, certifications
  • Browse and apply to relevant jobs
  • Track application status in real-time
  • Communicate via secure messaging
  • Post work progress updates
🛡️

Admin (Platform Manager)

  • Full user management and verification
  • Monitor all jobs and applications
  • Revenue and subscription analytics
  • Suspend or approve accounts
  • View platform-wide activity logs
  • Manage provider verification badges
🔐

Authentication & Security

  • Two-factor authentication (Email + OTP)
  • JWT-based stateless authentication
  • Role-based access control (RBAC)
  • Account lockout after 5 failed attempts
  • Trusted device support (skip OTP)
  • bcrypt password hashing (12 rounds)

Section 03

Implemented Features — Current Prototype

🔍 Job & Hiring System

  • Organizations post jobs with title, description, category, budget (NPR), deadline, district, urgency level, and required skills
  • Providers browse jobs with advanced filters: category, district, budget range, urgency, job type
  • Professional application form: cover letter, CV upload, portfolio links, expected salary, availability date
  • Application statuses: Pending → Shortlisted → Interview → Approved → Contracted → Rejected
  • AI-generated job descriptions based on category (Healthcare, Security, IT, etc.)

🤖 AI Smart Matching System

  • Match Score (0–100): Skills 40% + Rating 15% + Experience 15% + Location 10% + Trust Score 10% + Availability 5% + Verification 5%
  • Resume Score (0–100): Skills 40% + Experience 20% + Profile Completeness 20% + Portfolio 20%
  • Success Prediction: High / Medium / Low — based on resume score, track record, rating, and trust
  • Applications automatically sorted by highest score — organizations see best candidates first

📄 Contract & Workflow System

  • Digital contracts auto-generated from approved applications (org name, provider name, job title, amount, terms)
  • Digital signing by both parties — contract activates when both sign
  • Work progress timeline — providers post updates, organizations respond and approve
  • Contract history and status tracking (Draft → Signed → Active → Completed)

💬 Communication System

  • Real-time messaging powered by Socket.io — instant delivery without page refresh
  • Messaging unlocked only after application approval — prevents spam
  • 10 free messages per conversation, then premium unlock via payment
  • Real-time notifications for applications, approvals, contracts, and messages
  • Notification click navigation — each notification links to the relevant page

💳 Payment Integration

💜

Khalti Integration

Nepal's leading digital wallet. Payments processed in NPR via Khalti's e-Payment API v2. Amount sent in paisa for precision.

💚

eSewa Integration

Nepal's most widely used payment gateway. HMAC-SHA256 signature verification for secure transactions.

🆓

Free Plan

Basic profile, 5 applications/month, 10 free messages. No payment required.

⭐

Pro Plan — ₨150/month

Unlimited applications, messaging, verified badge, portfolio boost, contract generator.

Section 09

Conclusion

SkillForce — Transforming Nepal's Workforce Market

SkillForce Connects represents a significant step forward in modernizing how Nepal's organizations hire skilled service providers. By combining a professional digital workflow with AI-powered matching, real-time communication, digital contracts, and local payment integration, the platform addresses a genuine market gap that affects thousands of businesses and workers across the country.

The current prototype demonstrates a fully functional end-to-end hiring system — from job posting through to contract completion — built on a scalable, enterprise-grade technology stack. With planned enhancements including KYC verification, ML-based matching, mobile applications, and escrow payments, SkillForce is positioned to become Nepal's leading workforce marketplace.

The platform's subscription-based SaaS model, combined with commission-based revenue streams, provides a clear and sustainable path to commercial viability. SkillForce is not just a student project — it is a market-ready product with real potential to transform how Nepal's informal manpower economy operates.
